Privacy Policy

We have the utmost respect for our customers’ privacy. In no way will we violate this philosophy. We will not release any information gathered about our customers. Any information that we gather from our customers will be compiled anonymously and used solely for our statistical purposes.

The statistical information we gather from our customers will be used to help us enhance the experience for our customers. Any privacy concerns should be directed to wotton@roundtable.org.uk; we take all privacy concerns very seriously and will respond appropriately and quickly.

We may contact you directly for feedback on our event, if you’d prefer not to help us make the fireworks better please let us know.
